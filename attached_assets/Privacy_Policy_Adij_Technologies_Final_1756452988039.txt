## 5. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

### 5.1 Third-Party Service Providers
We share data with the following service providers under strict data processing agreements:

**OpenAI Services:**
- **OpenAI Whisper API:** Audio transcription services
- **OpenAI GPT-4o Mini:** Content enhancement and formatting
- **Purpose:** Core service functionality
- **Safeguards:** API key authentication, encrypted transmission, no long-term storage by OpenAI

**Infrastructure Services:**
- **Neon Database (PostgreSQL):** Hosted on Google Cloud Platform
- **Google Cloud Platform:** Database and storage infrastructure
- **Purpose:** Data storage and application hosting
- **Safeguards:** Enterprise-grade security, Indian data residency options

### 5.2 Cross-Border Data Transfer
Voice recordings are temporarily processed by OpenAI services located outside India. We implement:
- **Encryption in transit** using TLS 1.3 protocols
- **Contractual data processing agreements**
- **Limited processing duration** with deletion post-transcription
- **User consent** for cross-border processing
- **Note:** Transfer adequacy assessments ongoing as per DPDP Act implementation

### 5.3 Legal Disclosures
We may disclose data when required by:
- Valid legal process or court orders
- Government authorities under IT Act 2000
- Prevention of fraud or security threats
- Protection of our legal rights and property
- Child safety and protection requirements

## 6. DATA RETENTION

### 6.1 Standard Retention Period
- **Voice recordings:** Deleted after 1 (one) year from upload date
- **Transcribed content:** Retained until user account deletion
- **Account data:** Retained until account deletion plus 90 days for legal compliance
- **Minor data:** Shorter retention periods and additional protections

### 6.2 User-Controlled Retention
- **Explicit Save:** Users can explicitly save recordings/content indefinitely
- **Immediate Deletion:** Users can delete content anytime using account controls
- **Account Deletion:** All data permanently deleted within 30 days of account closure
- **Parental Control:** Parents can request immediate deletion of minor data

## 7. DATA SECURITY

### 7.1 Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **Encryption at Rest:** AES-256 encryption for stored data
- **Encryption in Transit:** TLS 1.3 for all data transmissions
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access with multi-factor authentication
- **Regular Security Audits:** Quarterly security assessments
- **Incident Response:** 24-hour breach notification protocols
- **Enhanced Minor Protection:** Additional safeguards for users under 18

### 7.2 Data Breach Response
In case of data breaches affecting personal data:
- **Immediate containment** within 1 hour of detection
- **Regulatory notification** as per DPDP Act requirements (when rules notified)
- **User notification** within 72 hours or as legally required, whichever is sooner
- **Remedial action** including security enhancement measures
